Round 1: Animbola - Tambola with a Twist

Attention Tambola enthusiasts and anime aficionados! Join us for the inaugural round, "AnimBola," where the world of Tambola meets the realm of anime. Round 2: Real-Life Snakes and Ladders

Set sail on an extraordinary adventure in our second round, "The Straw Hat Odyssey." Gather your crew and traverse the Grand Line in a real-life Snakes and Ladders game inspired by the One Piece anime. Navigate the game board filled with surprises, power-ups, and tributes to the Straw Hat crew. Will you conquer the challenges and reach the ultimate treasure, or will unexpected twists alter your course? Join us for a journey that mirrors the excitement and camaraderie of the One Piece anime.
